We’re thrilled to kick off the WEANing with LevoSimendan Study (WEANLESS)—a groundbreaking clinical trial aiming to help ICU patients breathe easier.
<br><br>
Respiratory muscle weakness often makes it tough for ventilated patients to come off breathing support. WEANLESS will test whether levosimendan, a drug known to boost muscle function, can shorten the time patients spend on ventilators.
<br><br>
We’re currently working on the final preparations with our partner hospitals to ensure everything is set for a smooth and successful start. Together, we’re committed to advancing critical care and improving patient outcomes.
<br><br>
By exploring this new approach, WEANLESS hopes to improve outcomes and quality of life for critically ill patients. Stay tuned for updates and check out the WEANLESS project page as the study gets underway!
